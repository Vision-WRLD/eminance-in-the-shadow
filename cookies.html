Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This site uses no cookies, no advertising pixels, and no tracking of any kind.

What we use instead

For convenience the site stores two small items in your browser's local storage, on your device only:

  • noct_bag — the pieces in your shopping bag.
  • noct_favs — the pieces you have favourited.

These never leave your browser and are not readable by us or any third party.

Clearing it

Clear your site data or local storage in your browser settings to remove both at any time. Removing your bag or favourites in the interface clears them too.

Third parties

Google Fonts and Unsplash deliver fonts and images and may log your IP for that delivery, but set no cookies through this site.
