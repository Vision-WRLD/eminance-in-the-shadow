Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This is a demonstration website. NOCTURNE is a fictional house created for portfolio purposes. No real products are sold and no payments are processed. This policy describes how the demo handles information should you enter any.

Information we handle

The site does not run a backend server. Anything you type, a search term, an email in the newsletter field, a checkout form, is processed entirely in your browser and is never transmitted to or stored on a server.

  • Favourites and bag contents are saved only in your browser's local storage on your own device.
  • Form submissions are simulated; no data leaves your browser.
  • No analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking scripts are loaded.

Third-party content

Fonts are served by Google Fonts and product imagery by Unsplash. These providers may receive your IP address as part of delivering that content. The video is served from this site.

Cookies

This site sets no cookies. It uses browser local storage for your bag and favourites, which you can clear at any time from your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy.

Your choices

Because nothing is collected server-side, there is nothing to request, correct, or delete on our end. Clearing your browser storage removes all locally saved data instantly.

Contact

Questions about this demo can be directed through the contact page.
